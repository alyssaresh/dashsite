Dashiell “Dash” Flach is an engineer, producer, and multi-instrumentalist living in Philadelphia, PA. He has toured the US playing keys, bass, and guitar, and is currently a member of the bands Lizdelise and Horror Movie Marathon. Dash has been engineering and producing music for over a decade, and his recorded work has been featured in a variety of publications including Rolling Stone, MTV, KEXP, Stereogum, and more.
